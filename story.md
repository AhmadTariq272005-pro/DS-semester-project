•	In the enchanted digital kingdom of Code Verse, a magical Text Editor faced chaos: words disappeared mysteriously, mistakes multiplied, and edit histories were lost.	Writers and coders grew frustrated, unable to trust their own creations as documents became unreliable. Suddenly, Linked List Hassan appeared first, connecting all words with invisible threads to ensure the text flowed smoothly. Hassan allowed seamless insertion, deletion, editing, and replacement, keeping every sentence complete and unbroken. Stack Ahmad arrived next, wielding his powerful LIFO magic.	With his abilities, Ahmad could instantly undo the most recent mistakes, restoring vanished words and correcting errors in reverse order. Queue Tabshair followed, carrying a magical scroll that recorded every action in perfect sequence. Tabshair patiently tracked every insertion, undo, and redo, creating a flawless timeline of edits that could never be erased. Together, Hassan, Ahmad, and Tabshair formed an unstoppable team: mistakes were fixed instantly, history was preserved perfectly, and words stayed connected flawlessly. Citizens of Code Verse celebrated as their magical Text Editor became the kingdom’s most reliable tool, making writing enchanting, effortless, and worry-free.
